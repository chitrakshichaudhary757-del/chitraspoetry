WHY I WRITE

I write when my heart gets too heavy to hold,
I write about my scars — both new and old.
When memories leave me in smiles and tears,
When some words haunt me and whisper my fears.

I write when I have a lot to share,
But don’t know how to express, and don’t wish to bare.
I write when I feel as lonely as one can be,
When silence speaks louder than any plea.

Not when tears, but when smiles disguise the pain,
I write to wash my soul in the rain.
When my lips tremble with a weary smile,
And my eyes, hiding the stories awhile.

I write to free the weight inside me,
To heal, to breathe, to simply be.
So ink becomes my secret friend,
And words — the wings on which I fly.

~Chitra
