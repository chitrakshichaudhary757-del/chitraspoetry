FROZEN IN TIME

As the roses lose their hue,
These petals get dry, but holds a clue.
Still it kept their faded beauty inside,
A love that blooms through the time flies.

Its wither petals holds a tale,
With the colors so vibrant, like a magic on nature’s scale.
A rose may fade and lose its bloom,
But it always dispells all the gloom.

Well, a fresh rose is always loved
For the fragrance of divine.
But the dried one — for the memories
Those are frozen in the time.

~ Chitra
