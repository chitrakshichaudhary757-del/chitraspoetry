SMILE

It’s your smile that captured my heart,
Every time I look at it, I feel we’ll never be apart.
The way your eyes light up, sparkling with joy,
Makes me feel like I’m your favorite — the one you enjoy.

I know, in that moment, you’re someone special,
Someone I want to hold and cherish forever.
Your smile is like a ray of sunshine on a rainy day,
It brightens me up in every single way.

It has the power to melt away all my worries,
And replace them with feelings of love and flurries.
Every time I see you, my heart skips a beat —
It’s like a melody, sweet and complete.

I know that I want to see it for the rest of my life.
You are my joy, my light —
And I’ll cherish your smile for all my life.

~Chitra
