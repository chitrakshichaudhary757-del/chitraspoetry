SOME WORDS

You said it in anger, I know. But that one word — it stayed with me.
Maybe for you, it was just a reaction. But for me,
it was something that went straight to my heart and refused to leave.
You moved on, but I’m still standing in the echo of that.
I’m not angry anymore — I’m just hurt.

Because when you know I’m the kind of person who feels everything deeply,
then why you said something that breaks me from inside?
I’m not asking for an apology, but atleast I deserve an explanation.
I just wish you could understand how heavy it still feels.

I smile and talk like normal,
but somewhere inside, that one word keeps replaying.
Maybe someday, I’ll stop hearing that echo inside me.
But today, it’s still here.
I hope you’ll understand…

~Chitra
